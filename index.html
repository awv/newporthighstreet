About This Project

Welcome to The History of Newport High Street, a digital archive exploring the changing face of our city's commercial heart.

This project uses historical records, trade directories, and census data to reconstruct the high street as it was from 1850 to the present day.

Created by: Newport Cornucopia

Data accuracy is paramount. If you spot an error, please contact us.
