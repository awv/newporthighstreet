STILL EXISTS

32 HIGH STREET

Positioned near the corner of Griffin Street, No. 32 has served as a cornerstone of Newport's commercial life for over 150 years.

KEY TENANTS

  • Thomas & Sons (Tailors) — 1892
  • The Golden Teapot — 1910
  • Evans & Co. — 1935

ABOUT THE BUILDING

A fine example of Victorian architecture, this property features original stonework and has maintained its facade despite internal modernisations.

INTERESTING FACTS

During the 1890s, this building was known for having one of the earliest telephone lines installed on the High Street.

HUMAN ELEMENT

The proprietor in 1901, James Evans, was a well-known local character who often sat on a stool outside the shop to greet passers-by.
